📖 About Project

Turning Data into Direction: The AI Startup Success Predictor

Every year, thousands of brilliant minds launch startups with the dream of changing the world. Yet, many startups fail within their first few years. The AI Startup Success Predictor uses Machine Learning to analyze startup data and estimate the probability of success.

🚀 Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to predict a startup's success probability using important business factors and provide data-driven insights for entrepreneurs and investors.

⚙️ How It Works

The machine learning model analyzes key startup metrics and identifies patterns that influence business growth and success.

📊 Key Factors Analyzed

  • Funding: Amount of financial support received.
  • Investors: Number of investors backing the startup.
  • Founders: Size and strength of the founding team.
  • Employees: Workforce size and operational capacity.
  • Startup Age: Maturity and growth stage of the company.

🎯 Why It Matters

  • Helps entrepreneurs evaluate business potential.
  • Supports investors in making informed decisions.
  • Provides data-driven insights to reduce risk.
  • Encourages strategic planning and growth.
